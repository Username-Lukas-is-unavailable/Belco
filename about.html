Our Alliance

The BELCO Alliance was initially collaboratively founded by Fontys UAS (the Netherlands), UCL University College (Denmark), CBS International Business School (Germany), BBA INSEEC (France) and LAB University of Applied Sciences (Finland), and has been growing into an interactive network between Higher Education Institutions worldwide.

Our Organization

Alliance at a glance

The BELCO Alliance was founded on a commitment to sustainability and quality through strategic partnerships. It aims to reshape international collaboration in higher education by developing programs that prepare culturally and economically aware professionals for the global workforce.

Alliance organization

The BELCO Alliance is built on a horizontal communication structure that promotes openness and collaboration among partners. Led by Dr. Maurice Cuypers, it emphasizes transparency, participation, and synergy to strengthen global cooperation.

BELCO Education & Accreditation

The BELCO Alliance develops programs for higher education in our BELCO Acceleration Academy. These modular programs are accessible to all partners in BELCO and this forms the foundation for our joint collaboration.
